Chapter 1

What is Takshashila?

Imagine an institution that bridges the gap between citizens and governance...

🏫

The Takshashila Institution, established in 2010, is an independent centre for research and education in public policy. It is a non-partisan, non-profit organisation based in Bengaluru that advocates the values of freedom, openness, tolerance, pluralism, and responsible citizenship.

🎯
Core Mission: To transform India through better public policies by developing better public servants, civil society leaders, professionals, and informed citizens.
2

Why "Takshashila"?

🏛️

Ancient Takshashila (Taxila) was the site of the world's oldest known university — a beacon of learning in the Indian subcontinent.

📜

It was here that Chanakya composed the Arthashastra, the foundational text of Indian statecraft and public policy.

"The ancient city of Takshashila was the intellectual fountainhead of Indian statecraft. The Takshashila Institution derives inspiration from that civilisational lighthouse in the pursuit of its contemporary objectives."

Did you know?

Ancient Takshashila attracted scholars from across Asia. Students of diverse backgrounds studied philosophy, medicine, law, military science, and — most importantly for the modern institution — the art of governance.

Chapter 3

The Policy School

The Takshashila Policy School offers multiple pathways to understanding public policy — from 12-week certificates to a full 48-week post-graduate programme.

📋

GCPP: Advanced Public Policy

12 weeks

Equips participants with tools to deepen understanding of economic and governance systems. Delivers clear frameworks for how politics and economics intersect.

Policy Analysis Economic Reasoning Governance
🛡️

GCPP: Defence & Foreign Affairs

12 weeks

Introduces geopolitics, strategic studies, and economic reasoning. Features a unique mix of military officers and civilians in every cohort.

Geopolitics Strategic Studies Foreign Policy
💻

GCPP: Technology & Policy

12 weeks

For technologists, lawyers, and policymakers. Covers economics of tech platforms, political economy, ethical orientation, and public discourse leadership.

Tech Platforms AI Policy Digital Governance

We, The Citizens

We, The
Citizens Strengthening the
Indian Republic

What is a democracy?
How is a republic different from a monarchy?

Chapter 7

Impact & Initiatives

📡

Anticipating the Unintended

A weekly newsletter on public policy ideas and frameworks, exploring the unseen consequences of policy decisions.

🎙️

Puliyabaazi Podcast

A popular Hindi-Urdu podcast on politics, policy, and technology — making policy discourse accessible in Indian languages.

💬

Tankscrub

An in-house weekly discussion on policy topics, held every Thursday since inception — the heartbeat of intellectual discourse at Takshashila.

🎪

Shalas

Unconferences with an innovative, informal format held across India — bringing policy discussions out of boardrooms and into communities.

🏢

Executive Programmes

Specialised programmes for corporate professionals, including Strategic Government Engagement for corporate executives (launched with the Tata Group).
